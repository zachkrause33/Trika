four ways to play
Trika
Spot equations in the grid. Beat the clock. Build your streak.
Trika Blitz
45 seconds flat. Find as many equations as you can.
Trika Precision
6 equations. Each result adds to your sum. Land as close to 33 as you can.
Trika Survival
Numbers fall. Tap any three to form an equation. Don't let the board fill up.
