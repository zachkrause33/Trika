How to play
1
Numbers fall into the board one at a time. The speed increases over time.
2
Tap any three tiles on the board in order to form an equation. They don't need to be adjacent — tap them in equation order: 4 → 2 → 2 = 4÷2=2 ✓
3
Cleared tiles vanish. Game over when the board is completely full.
